About Us

We are a small community with mostly young people who share the same passion which is playing badminton. At first it consisted of only friends from the same village, then added members from other regions, whom they already knew.

We used to hold regular exercises every Thursday night in a building located in Gadingan, Mojolaban, Sukoharjo. We also often hang out together after regular practice which is to build a relationship to be closer to each other. We also travel together to just refresh us.
